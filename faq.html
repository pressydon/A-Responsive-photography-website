Frequently Asked Questions

Do you travel?

Yes, We travel and we would love to discuss travel details with you.

How Much more do you edit your pictures ?

Post-production is the most time concentrated part of your event/shoot as we work extremely hard to ensure your images meet our standards.

How do we book you?

After going through our website, you can book by clicking on the book button on the homepage or contact us through our social media pages via instagram DM, whatsapp or email address. You can also reach out to us through the above medium listed above for further inquiry and recommendation. We respond within 48hours. We offer in person and over the phone consultations to discuss bookings. A 70% non-refundable retainer fee for date reservation and both signatures are required to reserve our services for your date. Ones the retainer is paid we start to reject any event that falls on that day .

Can you hold date for us??

Unfortunately, we can not hold date without monetary commitments and payment receipts has been acknowledged .

Are we to provide accommodation?

Yes, You will provide hotel accommodation which covers event nights outside Abuja. Prefarably the same hotel where the event is happening or in close proximity to that of the event as this will make it easy for us to work with you. This practice serves best for weddings so we can take lovely portraits of the couple and their main train before heading for church .

How long does it take to get our Images?

In at most 2weeks from the date of the shoot, the pictures should be ready, But we understand that some clients book us for pre-birthday shoot,We will have some ready before the birthday for your use if there is a reasonable enough time to work with.

Do you deliver raw pictures?

We only give out raw picture at cost. Every photo you recieve will be edited to ensure consistency in colors, exposure, and mood reflective of our current style.
