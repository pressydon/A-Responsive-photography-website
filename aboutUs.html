About Us

Precious Photographs is a registered company based in Abuja, Federal Capital Territory, Nigeria and a team of talented individuals with enviable professional experience in photography, cinematic storytelling, videography, art designs, creativity and event management. The images we create are precious to us and so are our Clients, so we stop at nothing to give you that beautifully memorable experience you desire. At Precious Photographs, our creativity goes beyond a lifetime and our pictures are ageless. Our areas of expertise include wedding photography and video coverage, landscape and nature photography, in-studio and outdoor photography, fashion photography, and all kinds of event photo and video coverage.

Creative Director

This photography superstar generates photographic content for big brands and exposes his intimacy with photography in all his pieces.
